Dividing Fractions

Did you convert it to an improper fraction?

If you have a mixed number, you have to convert it to a mixed fraction first. Multiply the denomiator and whole number, then add the numerator. Remember the denominator stays the same.
Warning – Put a 1 under a whole number to turn it into a fraction. You're 11-years-old? 11/1 would be your age as a fraction.

If you have an improper fraction (larger on top), you have to convert it to a mixed number first.

Did you write the problem correctly?

If you think you did everything correctly, double check to make sure you wrote the problem down correctly. Also, make sure you can read your handwriting. If your 4s look like 9s, you probably wrote the wrong number.
