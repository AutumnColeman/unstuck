Did you multiply and add correctly?

Most of the multiplication problems are wrong because of simple mistakes. Double check your multiplication and addition to make sure it's right. It's best to write it again so you can compare it to your first problem.

Did you draw your loops?

Don't do all the work and get it wrong because you put the decimal in the wrong place. Draw your loops and count how many times you will need to move the decimal in your answer.

Did write the problem correctly?

If you think you did everything correctly, double check to make sure you wrote the problem down correctly. Also, make sure you can read your handwriting. If your 4s and 9s look alike, you probably wrote the wrong number.
